How to Make a Payment

Please follow the steps below to complete your payment using mobile money services.

M-Pesa (Vodacom)

To pay using M-Pesa, follow these steps:

  1. Dial *150*00#
  2. Select 4: Pay by M-Pesa
  3. Select Business Number
  4. Enter Business Number: 123456
  5. Enter Reference Number: Your Booking ID
  6. Enter Amount
  7. Enter PIN to confirm

Tigo Pesa

To pay using Tigo Pesa, follow these steps:

  1. Dial *150*01#
  2. Select 4: Pay Bill
  3. Select Business Number
  4. Enter Business Number: 654321
  5. Enter Reference Number: Your Booking ID
  6. Enter Amount
  7. Enter PIN to confirm

Airtel Money

To pay using Airtel Money, follow these steps:

  1. Dial *150*60#
  2. Select 5: Make Payment
  3. Enter Business Number: 987654
  4. Enter Reference Number: Your Booking ID
  5. Enter Amount
  6. Enter PIN to confirm

Halopesa

To pay using Halopesa, follow these steps:

  1. Dial *150*88#
  2. Select 4: Pay Bill
  3. Enter Business Number: 567890
  4. Enter Reference Number: Your Booking ID
  5. Enter Amount
  6. Enter PIN to confirm

After completing the payment, keep the transaction ID as proof of payment.
